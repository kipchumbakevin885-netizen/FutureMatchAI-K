# рџљЂ FutureMatch AI

> **Empowering TVET Students Through Intelligent Career Matching**

FutureMatch AI is an AI-powered career guidance and employability platform designed to bridge the gap between Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET) institutions and the labour market in Kenya.

The platform helps students discover suitable career paths, identify skill gaps, receive personalized learning recommendations, and connect with internships and employment opportunities based on their skills, interests, assessment results, and labour-market demand.

---

# рџЊЌ Problem Statement

Many TVET graduates possess valuable practical skills but struggle to secure internships and employment due to:

* Limited career guidance
* Poor visibility to employers
* Skills mismatches
* Lack of personalized career recommendations
* Difficulty transitioning from education to employment

Traditional job platforms primarily focus on experienced professionals and keyword-based job searches, leaving many first-time job seekers underserved.

FutureMatch AI addresses these challenges through intelligent, skills-based career matching.

FutureMatch AI provides an intelligent digital platform that enables students to:

* Build stronger connections with employers

Employers can also discover talent based on demonstrated competencies rather than relying solely on academic qualifications or previous work experience.

# рџ¤– AI Recommendation Engine

FutureMatch AI currently uses a rule-based recommendation engine designed to simulate intelligent career matching.

Career recommendations are generated using:

* Skills (40%)
* Interests (30%)
* Course Relevance (15%)
* Assessment Results (15%)

The recommendation engine also:

* Identifies missing competencies
* Generates personalized learning pathways
* Matches students with careers aligned to labour-market demand

The architecture has been designed to support future integration with machine learning models and cloud-based AI services without requiring major frontend changes.
