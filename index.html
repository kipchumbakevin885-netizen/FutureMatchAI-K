TVET graduates are talented.

The Problem

Thousands of TVET students graduate every year without a clear path from classroom to career.

  • No visibility into which skills employers actually need
  • Career guidance is generic, not personalised
  • Internships and jobs are scattered across disconnected channels
  • Students don't know which skills to build next

The FutureMatch Solution

One AI-powered platform that turns a student's skills and interests into a concrete career plan.

  • AI-matched careers ranked by real fit, not guesswork
  • Personalised learning roadmaps to close skill gaps
  • A single place to find internships and jobs that match
  • Live labour-market demand woven into every recommendation
